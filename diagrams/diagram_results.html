Hyperparameter Sensitivity — Qualitative Summary
γ — Discount Factor
High required for sparse rewards. Too low → undercredits future deliveries. Too high → destabilizes via over-discounting immediates.
α — Learning Rate
Double-edged. Too low → slow convergence. Too high → overestimation bias, Q-value divergence.
τ — Target Smoothing
Too small → lagging targets → uninformative TD signal. Balance needed across all layout complexities.
λ — ε-Decay Rate ⚡ CRITICAL
Phase transition behavior — not a smooth tradeoff. Too slow: noise dominates. Too fast: premature commitment to suboptimal equilibria.
